Fascinating Points of Kashmir

Leepa Valley

Leepa Valley is a breathtakingly beautiful valley located in Azad Kashmir, often referred to as "Mini Kashmir"

Muzaffarabad

Muzaffarabad is the capital of Azad Kashmir in Pakistan. It is located on the banks of the Jhelum and Neelum rivers.

Neelum Valley

Neelum Valley is situated at the North & North-East of Muzaffarabad Azad Kashmir, running parallel to Kaghan Valley.
